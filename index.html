About SYDP

A Long-Term Pathway,
Not a One-Off Training

SYDP is a structured youth empowerment and enterprise development initiative designed to identify, nurture, mentor, and support talented young people in transforming their natural abilities into productive economic assets.

Talent› Skill› Product› Enterprise› Wealth› Impact

Programme Objectives

✓Identify talented & innovative students
✓Profile talents, skills & economic potential
✓Develop entrepreneurial competencies
✓Create enterprise pathways
✓Promote sector-based collaboration
✓Provide long-term mentorship
✓Connect participants with investors
✓Build a sustainable ecosystem

Vision

To build Africa's leading university-based youth development ecosystem that transforms talents into enterprises, leaders, innovators, and job creators.

Mission

To identify, profile, develop, mentor, and support young people in converting their talents and innovative ideas into sustainable enterprises and impactful careers.

The Maiden Edition

UNILAG Cohort 1 (July 2026) is co-created by Auxilium Max Company Limited (AMC), Lead Implementor, and BlackBelt Global Consulting Limited, Strategic Partner & Co-Delivery Lead — the proof-of-concept for Pan-African expansion.

How to Join

Student Selection Process

A rigorous, faculty-led process ensures we identify truly motivated, high-potential participants from across the University of Lagos.

01

Nomination

Twelve departments nominate through Department Presidents, Class Representatives & Faculty Leadership. Approximately 1,000 students identified.

02

Screening

Students screened on CV, character, leadership potential, academic standing, and peer recommendations. 600 students selected.

03

Registration

Selected students complete a digital profile capturing talents, skills, enterprise interests, leadership experience & mentorship preferences.

Programme Structure

Three Stages. One Year.
A Lifetime of Impact.

Every participant has a pathway. No one is left behind — from full incubation for the top teams to an alternative track for everyone else.

Stage 1 — Months 1–2

Business Excellence Orientation & Pitching

All 600 participants undergo structured training across 11 modules covering mindset, leadership, financial literacy, marketing, project management & pitch development.

  • 11 modules, 10 marks each (100 total)
  • Business project submitted as assignment
  • Top 50 teams present at Internal Pitch Day
  • Top 10 selected for full incubation + prizes
  • Next 40 teams: CAC registration + support
📅 2 – 28 Jul 2026 Online + UNILAG
Stage 2 — Months 2–4

Incubation & Investment Readiness

The Top 10 winning teams enter a structured incubation and acceleration track, co-delivered by AMC and BlackBelt Global Consulting Limited.

  • Scholarships for advanced business training
  • Facilities Management operational excellence
  • Direct enterprise set-up or expansion funding
  • BOI-checklist coaching & bankable plans
  • Investment Matchmaking Demo Day
🏆 Top 10 Teams Full Incubation
Stage 3 — Months 5–12

Alternative Incubation Track

Remaining participants enrol in a PALMS Africa-led development track — preparing them to return to the next SYDP cohort as stronger, better-prepared teams.

  • Strategic leadership excellence training
  • Innovative entrepreneurship coaching
  • Quality Control mentoring sessions
  • Preparation for SYDP Cohort 2
  • Access to BlackBeltCEO Business Clinic
🌱 No One Left Behind PALMS Africa Track
